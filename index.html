What is Chroneza?

Chroneza is a web application designed to help students create and manage their time-tables effectively. It allows users to input their subjects, classes, and study hours, and generates a personalized time-table that can be easily accessed and ideally followed.



Feature Description
Customizable Time-Table Users can create a time-table that fits their unique schedules and preferences.
Subject Management Add, edit, or remove subjects and classes as needed.
Study Hours Tracking Keep track of study hours to ensure balanced learning.
Downloadable Time-Tables Users can Download and Print their Unique Schedules.
